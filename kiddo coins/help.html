Frequently Asked Questions

What is Kiddo Coins?

Kiddo Coins is a platform where kids can complete tasks assigned by their parents and earn virtual coins as rewards.

How can I earn Kiddo Coins?

Kids can earn Kiddo Coins by completing tasks assigned to them. Each completed task rewards 100 coins.

How do I upload a task completion video?

After completing a task, navigate to the task page and use the upload button to submit your task completion video.

Can I redeem my Kiddo Coins?

Yes, you can redeem your Kiddo Coins for exciting rewards through the "Redeem" section of the platform.
